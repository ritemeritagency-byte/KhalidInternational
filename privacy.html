Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

Khalid International Recruitment Agency values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and application form.

How We Use Your Information

  • To process your recruitment inquiry and application
  • To connect you with assigned support specialists
  • To coordinate follow-ups and deployment-related guidance

Data Sharing

We only share your information with authorized recruitment personnel and support staff for legitimate processing of your application.

Data Security

We apply reasonable organizational and technical safeguards to protect your submitted information from unauthorized access and misuse.

Your Rights

You may request correction or update of your submitted information by contacting our support team through our official WhatsApp channel.
