FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Quick Answers for Applicants

Is there a placement fee?

No. Khalid International follows a no placement fee policy for applicants.

What countries do you deploy to?

We currently process opportunities for UAE Dubai, Qatar, and Kuwait.

Do I need a passport before applying?

You can apply even without a passport yet. Our team will guide you on next steps.

How long does processing take?

Processing time depends on position, documents, and employer requirements. We provide updates at every step.

How will I receive updates?

Your assigned specialist will contact you via WhatsApp or phone for interview and document updates.

Where is your office located?

Our office is at 2269 Nakar St., San Andres Bukid, Manila, Philippines.
